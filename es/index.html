What Could Go Wrong

Crypto investing can be volatile, which means prices can change fast in either direction. Platform failures, policy shifts, and crowded trades can also hurt outcomes.

  • We use size limits to reduce single-position damage.
  • We use staged entries instead of all-in timing.
  • We use clear exit triggers when momentum weakens.

What it means: risk cannot be removed, but it can be controlled.
